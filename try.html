Easy Steps to Grow at Home

  1. Choose the Right Pot & Soil: Use a medium-sized pot (8-12 inches deep) with drainage holes. Fill with well-draining soil mixed with compost.
  2. Planting the Seeds or Cuttings: From Seeds: Soak seeds in water overnight before planting. From Cuttings: Use a 4-6 inch stem cutting from a healthy plant.
  3. Sunlight & Temperature: Place the plant in a sunny location (4-6 hours of sunlight daily). Prefers warm temperatures (20-30Â°C).
  4. Watering: Keep the soil moist but not soggy. Water 2-3 times a week (reduce in winter).
  5. Fertilization & Maintenance: Use organic compost or cow dung manure once a month. Trim the plant regularly to encourage bushy growth.
